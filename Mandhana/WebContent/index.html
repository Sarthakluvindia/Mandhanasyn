ABOUT US

Mandhana Synthetics Private Limited (MSPL) is a textile manufacturing company incorporated in the year 1986. MSPL has been engaged in the manufacturing of cotton and blended fabrics of various types over the last 25 years. Excellence and customer satisfaction are our driving factors, hence you can always be assured of high quality products at competitive prices.

We have manufacturing facilities at Ichalkaranji (Maharashtra) and Ahmedabad. Currently we are a major supplier to some known textile companies and some known exporters in India.

We also design, manufacture, retail and distribute our range of bed linens and other products under the brand name of “Mandhana Tex Mills”.Our wide product portfolio includes all types of fabrics required for hospitality, home textile, institutional and general Textiles.

Products

Mandhana Synthetics Pvt. Ltd. market their product under the name of Mandhana Text Mills. The product range includes the best of home essential fabrics from bed to bath.

Premium Stripes

100% pure cotton is blended to make our premium stripes, Petals. They are available in 210TC and 300TC with 20 shades in each.

Bath Essentials

With the best absorbing fabrics like terrycot we manufacture our bath essentials for home. They include bath towels, hand towels, bathroom floormat, turkish premium towels and beach towels.

Quality

As our motto suggests, we are the ultimate destination for superior quality fabrics and finished products and in order to maintain this standard for our product, we make it undergo various quality checks at each stage of manufacturing.

The fabric is under continuous supervision during various stages of processing and undergoes testing for the same, this reduces the chance for unobserved defect in the fabric under process.

Our garment unit is equipped with the best in class Juki machines which gives us a superior market finish for all our products. The fabric has to undergo a final quality check in our cut looking department where experienced masters approve the product and it is further sent for packaging and dispatch. Hence, we make sure to provide you with highest quality fabric that meets the most demanding customer’s quality requirement.
